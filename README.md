# Mabini-Travelers

Mabini Travelers is a responsive travel guide website showcasing the top destinations and activities in Mabini, Batangas. It features:

🏖️ Beaches – Perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and beach adventures.

🏨 Resorts – Relax in comfortable beachfront resorts and villas.

⛰️ Mountains – Scenic hiking spots with breathtaking views.

🤿 Diving Spots – Explore Anilao’s world-class coral reefs and marine life.

The site includes a carousel hero section, category cards, and a “Plan Your Trip” section with links to hotels, activities, and transport services. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it highlights the natural beauty of Mabini while providing users with helpful trip-planning options.
